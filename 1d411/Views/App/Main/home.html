Seed database

Seeding the database with orders and shipments takes a long time because of the huge amount of rows in the databases. To seed use the URLs below.

Ordrers one month at the time
/diagrams/seed-orders-for/{year:int}/{month:int}

All orders(since 2007), several hundred thousands, probably takes more than an hour.
(inactivated)/diagrams/seed-all-live-orders

Shipments on month at the time:
/diagrams/seed-shipments-for/{year:int}/{month:int}

All shipments(since 2007), several hundred thousands, probably takes more than an hour.
(inaktiverad)/diagrams/seed-all-live-shipments
